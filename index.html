Binary Decisions

Binary decisions is an easy way to sort tasks or activities in order of importance/urgency by creating a set of decisions for you to make between two options at a time.

It can also be used to rank your all-time favorite movies or to discover who your favorite artist is!

Enter up to 10 options, press start, and work through the binary decisions to get to receive your results.
